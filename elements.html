My Collaborators

My PhD supervisor and mentors

Emilian Dudas

From 2012 to 2015, Emilian was my PhD supervisor at the CPHT of École Polytechnique. This was an incredible experience, I learned so much about so many things with him, and would certainly recommend Emilian as a supervisor to any interested student!

Yann Mambrini

During my PhD, although he was not my supervisor, Yann played a big role in helping me discover the world of astroparticles and dark matter phenomenology. Yann is a very project-oriented researcher, with a fantastic energy. He has always been supportive and I will never thank him enough for all the time and ideas he shared with me during and beyond the PhD.

Wilfried Buchmüller

As I visited DESY for over a year during my PhD, I had the chance to collaborate with Wilfried on several projects. Wilfried has treated me like his own PhD student at the time with a remarkable kindness. I learned a lot with him about supergravity and cosmic inflation.
